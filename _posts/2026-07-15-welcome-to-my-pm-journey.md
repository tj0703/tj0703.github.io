Welcome! This blog is my public notebook on learning Product Management. I'm starting from the beginning — no PM title, no certifications yet, just curiosity and a plan. Every week I'll share what I studied, what confused me, and what finally made sense. If you're also at the start of this road, I hope my notes make your path a little shorter. And if you're further along: tell me what I'm getting wrong. That's half the reason I'm writing in public.
